...

Contemporary students in Canadian post-secondary institutions are increasingly burdened with debt, stress and consequentially poor health. A cohesive strategy is necessary to provide students with a comprehensive network of services to help promote well being, reduce stress, and enhance the student experience.

U of T Health Connect began as an advocacy project for an undergraduate course in the University of Torontoâ€™s Health Studies program. Students identified the fragmented integration among mental health and well being groups on campus. The goal of U of T Health Connect is to provide a network of services and organizations within the university, both student and faculty run.
